retroPod

Android music player built to look like my 5th generation iPod Video

The app will show you all the music on your device, sorted by Artist, Album, and a list of all the songs. The click wheel works just like how you remember, with haptic feedback while scrolling. You can adjust the clickwheel clicker, shuffle songs, and/or repeat all options in the settings.

The app was built over Thanksgiving week 2019 after seeing this inspiration from @elvin_not_11
