Vinery

Vinery was an application for the Vine video sharing service.

Released before the official app, Vinery was a full third party client which allowed you to view videos, like/comment, and update your profile.

Within the first month the application received over two hundred and fifty thousand downloads, was ranked in the trending section on the play store and appeared in the top 25 free social applications for android.
