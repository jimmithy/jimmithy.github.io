Digital Doily

As you start drawing, your lines are replicated to generate cool pieces of art. Adjust the number of segments, colors, shapes, and line widths to create your own one of a kind rangoli, mandala, or spirograph.

I saw this post on /r/whoadude about an app called Amaziograph and was impressed as someone threw together a javascript version within a few hours. Easily tempted as ever, I spent a few nights coming up with an android version. Since it's release, I've been amazed by the fantastic works of art created.
